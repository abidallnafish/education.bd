Educational Categories

Structured and systematic, formal education takes place in schools, colleges, and universities. It follows a set curriculum and leads to certification or degrees, providing foundational knowledge and skills.

Learning that occurs outside formal institutions, informal education is unstructured and spontaneous. It includes skills and knowledge acquired through daily experiences, social interactions, and self-directed activities.

Organized and intentional, non-formal education occurs outside the traditional school system. It includes programs like adult education, community-based learning, and vocational training, focusing on practical skills.

Tailored to meet the unique needs of individuals with disabilities or learning differences, special education provides personalized instruction and support. It aims to ensure that all students have access to education.
